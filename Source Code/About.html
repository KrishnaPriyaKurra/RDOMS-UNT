Welcome to RDOMS


Residential Dining Operations Management System at UNT


It goes without saying as to how much food is wasted on a daily basis throughout the world. Most of this food could be utilized fruitfully if proper measures are taken. We have observed the same in the UNT residential dining scenario as well. Every semester/year a number of students register for meal plans but not all utilize it efficiently. This could be because some might feel the dining halls are too far away; might be too lazy to go; might be wanting to eat from multiple dining halls at once but ultimately end up ordering from other restaurants, and the list goes on. Consequently, the dining halls undergo a lot of wastage of food almost every day.

Our website RDOMS solely to address this issue- is an opertaions management system for UNT Residentail Dining which allows the residential students of UNT, and the managers representing the residential dining halls come together to perform a wide range of activities. On one hand the website has a student interface which allows students to access the menus of UNT residential dining services, customize their orders from multiple dining halls in a single order and place it for delivery to the students preferred dining hall. The studnets can check-in to the dining halls prior to their arrival, participate in polls and suggest chnages to the existing menus.

On the other hand the website also has a manager interface, which allows the managers of the respective dining halls to plan ahead according to the web check-in and a Food Dispersal System to utilize the food efficiently before expiration.

Our approach mainly focuses on the following principle:
“Give what the customer wants”.
Ultimately, all these smaller solutions cumulatively help in alleviating the food wastage issue at UNT Residential Dining, even if it is just a bit.
